Technical Courses:

ABBR is a well-established institute that has been offering a wide range of professional courses to dedicated and aspiring students for decades. In addition to vocational programs such as fashion designing, ABBR also provides comprehensive technical training. Our technical courses cover a variety of in-demand fields, including:

Typewriting

Learn efficient and accurate typing skills for office and clerical work.

CCTV Camera Repairing and Installation

Master installation and troubleshooting of CCTV systems for security setups.

AutoCAD & Designing

Gain skills in computer-aided design for engineering and architecture.

Smartphone Mechanism (Hardware)

Learn to repair and service smartphones including chip-level training.

Computer Hardware and Networking

Understand computer components and build secure network systems.

Tally (Accounting Packages)

Master financial accounting using Tally and related software tools.

Customer Relations and Sales

Enhance your interpersonal skills and sales techniques for business growth.

What makes our courses beneficial for students?

We offer a wide range of technical courses under one roof at affordable prices. Each course is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge, combining both theoretical concepts and hands-on practical training. All sessions are conducted by seasoned professionals with years of industry experience. Our instructors are committed to helping every student become proficient in their chosen field. The classes are structured in a well-organized and synchronized manner to enhance the learning experience. Equipped practical labs and modern tools are available to support skill development. Additionally, students receive job-oriented training alongside regular coursework, ensuring they are industry-ready upon completion.
