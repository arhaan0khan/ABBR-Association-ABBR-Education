Professional Courses:

"Since 2004, ABBR has been a well-recognized institute dedicated to skill development. With over a decade of experience, the institute continues to offer a diverse range of professional courses. Some of its most popular training programs focus on empowering individuals with in-demand vocational skills."

Garment Making & Fashion Designing

Learn the art of garment making and become skilled in fashion designing techniques.

Basic Sewing Machine Operating & Hand Embroidery

Master the use of sewing machines and hand embroidery techniques for creative work.

Beautician and Mehandi Designing

Learn the essentials of beautician work and intricate Mehandi (Henna) designs.

Zari and Zardozi Works

Master the art of traditional Indian embroidery techniques, Zari and Zardozi works.

Curtain Making & Designing

Learn to design and craft beautiful curtains to enhance the decor of any space.

Original Flower Arrangement and Bouquets with Material

Create beautiful flower arrangements and bouquets using high-quality materials.

All Types of Painting

Learn different painting techniques, including oil painting, watercolors, and more.

"All the above courses are conducted by expert faculty members with years of hands-on experience, offering continuous guidance and supervision. Every student receives individual attention, ensuring a personalized learning experience. These courses provide a great opportunity for learners to discover and sharpen their talents. After training, students can choose to start their own ventures, join fashion houses, boutiques, or established companies. They can also pursue freelance work or even become instructors in their field. The possibilities are limitless—what’s needed is passion, dedication, and the courage to dream big."
