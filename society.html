Our Key Initiatives

Free vocational training, career guidance, soft skills development, free SSC coaching, digital literacy, and physical education programs to uplift underprivileged communities.

From vocational awareness seminars, AIDS camps, to skill-building programs and government job coaching, ABBR has consistently delivered community-focused initiatives with guaranteed placement support and empowerment.
